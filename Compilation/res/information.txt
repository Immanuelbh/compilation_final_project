1.  I added the ' _CRT_SECURE_NO_WARNINGS'  flag to the Preprocess Definitions through the projects property pages
To replicate: 
Left click on the project in Visual Studio ->
Click on Properties ->
Under 'Configuration Properties' click on 'C/C++' and click on 'Preprocessor' ->
Click on the 'Preprocessor Definitions editbox, press on the small down-arrow and click on '<Edit...>' ->
Now paste '_CRT_SECURE_NO_WARNINGS' in the first editbox ->
Press OK and then OK again to save and return to the project.
